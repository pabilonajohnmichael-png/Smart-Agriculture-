Problem

Farmers often struggle to monitor their crops regularly. Issues like pests, lack of water, and poor soil can affect crop growth. Without monitoring, harvest production may decrease.

Solution

The Smart Agriculture Monitoring System helps farmers track their crops, manage activities, and access farming tips to improve productivity.
